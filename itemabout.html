Loot Sim


This is a tool I made to simulate loot drops.

1 Item: Simulate obtaining one item at any drop rate.
2 Items: Simulate obtaining two items at any drop rate.
3 Items: Simulate obtaining three items at any drop rate.

Item: The item you are rolling for.
Goal: How many drops of the item you want.
Rate: Drop rate of the item.
Iterations: How many simulations do you want to run?
